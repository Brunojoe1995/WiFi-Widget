Widget configuration options include:
- <i>Color scheme, where you may select between dynamic light/dark mode, static light/dark mode, or a completely customizable one</i>
- <i>Background opacity</i>
- <i>Connection properties to be displayed</i>
- <i>Widget buttons to be included</i>
- <i>Data refreshing parameters, including whether to display the 'last refreshed' date time</i>
- <i>Widget size</i>

WiFi connection properties displayed by the widget:
- <i>SSID</i>
- <i>BSSID</i>
- <i>Local IPv4 address, including prefix length and additional information</i>
- <i>Local & public IPv6 addresses, including prefix length and additional information</i>
- <i>Frequency</i>
- <i>Channel</i>
- <i>Link Speed</i>
- <i>Gateway</i>
- <i>DNS</i>
- <i>DHCP</i>
